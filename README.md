Silaris Agentic AI — Quality & Training Intelligence Centre
An AI-powered call quality monitoring and training management platform built for BPO operations managing outbound insurance sales for Axis Max Life Insurance.
Overview
This platform replaces traditional manual call auditing (3-5% sampling) with 100% automated call analysis using Speech-to-Text (STT) and AI-driven Call Quality Index (CQI) scoring. It monitors every call, identifies agent skill gaps, auto-generates personalized training, and tracks improvement — with human intervention as the fallback, not the default.
Key Capabilities

100% Call Coverage — Every call analyzed via STT, not sampled
6-Dimension CQI Scoring — Communication, Product Knowledge, Objection Handling, Customer Handling, Closing, Compliance
AI-Generated Training — Personalized coaching plans delivered to agent desktops within hours of gap detection
Agentic Co-Pilot — Contextual AI suggestions with approve/review workflow across every screen
Training Lifecycle Tracking — 7-stage pipeline from gap detection through assessment and outcome
Competition Intelligence — Feature-benefit analysis vs HDFC Life, SBI Life, ICICI Prudential based on actual customer call data
Voice of Customer — Verbatim customer scenarios in Hindi-English with attribution analysis
Compliance Monitoring — Fatal/non-fatal error detection, IRDAI readiness scoring, CAP (Corrective Action Plan) tracking

Architecture

Role-Based Access — Agent, Team Leader, Assistant Manager, Manager, Quality Leader, Training Lead, Training Manager, AVP & Above, Competition Analysis
Top Navigation — Process Overview (Home), Call Analytics, Scenarios & Objections, Voice of Customer
Co-Pilot Sidebar — Persistent AI assistant with role-specific insights and actionable suggestions
Artifact Modals — Every approve/review action opens a contextual execution report with impact metrics, timeline, and audit trail
